Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data Lynx collects when a short link is followed.

What we collect

When you follow a Lynx short link, the following is recorded:

  • Timestamp: the date and time of the access.
  • User-agent string: the browser or client identifier sent automatically by your browser (e.g. "Chrome/124", "Firefox/125").

What we do not collect

  • IP addresses
  • Location data
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers for visitors
  • Any personal account information

Why we collect it

The timestamp and user-agent are used solely to provide the owner of this Lynx instance with aggregate analytics (for example, how many times a link was followed on a given day and which browsers were used). This data is never sold or shared with third parties.

How long it is kept

Access records are stored for as long as the corresponding short link exists. Deleting a link removes all associated access records.

Self-hosted software

Lynx is self-hosted, open-source software. The operator of this instance is responsible for how data is handled. If you have questions about this specific deployment, contact the person or organisation who shared the link with you.
